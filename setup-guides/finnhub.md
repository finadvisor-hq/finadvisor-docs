# Finnhub Setup Guide
*Last verified: June 2026*

Finnhub provides free real-time stock market data for FinAdvisor.

## Steps

1. Open your browser and go to **finnhub.io/register**
2. Enter your email address and create a password
3. Check your email for a confirmation link and click it
4. Log in — your API key appears on the dashboard home page
5. Copy the key and paste it into FinAdvisor Settings → Market Data

## What it does

Finnhub provides live prices for SPY, ICE, DOW, QQQ, and BTC.
Your investment values update automatically during market hours.

## Cost

Free forever. 60 API calls per minute. No credit card required.
